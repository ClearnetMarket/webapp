Answers

I never received my item.

Back to top
If you didn't receive your item, or cant find it.
  • Verify the tracking says delivered
  • Check for notices left around the house
  • Wait 36 hours, sometimes carriers mark as delivered a day early
  • Contact your carrier directly

My item is taking a long time.

Back to top
If the item was mark as shipped, the tracking will provide the progress. If the tracking is blank, the vendor has not delivered it to the carrier yet.

The vendor didn't provide tracking.

Back to top
If a vendor did not provide tracking, you can ask him for it. If he doesnt have it, then you are still protected under our 30 day protection.

The tracking is showing issues with the carrier.

Back to top
Weather, delivery issues, and other problems can slow down your package. You can request the vendor send another one, and issue a return if your shows up.

Can I get faster shipping?

Back to top
The vendor states in his shipping options if there is options for speed. It doesnt hurt to directly message and ask for other options. Vendors usually try to accommodate there customers
