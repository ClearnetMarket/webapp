Answers

Can I return an item I bought?

Back to top
Yes you are allowed to return an item provided it meets these requirements:
  • The item was listed as returnable
  • You are not past the 14 day return time.

Why do so few vendors and Clearnet Market not allow returns?

Back to top
Due the fluctuation of cryptocurrencies, returning items can be profitable to buyers. Example: They buy an item when bitcoin is 5000$ a coin. Bitcoin goes to 10000$ a coin. If the buyer initiates a return, then they get back coin worth twice as much. Due to this, we do not allow returns to Clearnet Market and guide vendors to not do the same.

How do items bought from Clearnet Market that needed to be returned work?

Back to top
If the Item is listed as an amazon item, then the item was shipped as a gifted item. Gifted items can be returned to Amazon via there return system. Contact Amazon for customer service.

How long do I have to return an item?

Back to top
From the time you place your order, you have 14 days.

What if the item is past the return time?

Back to top
If the shipping is taking a long time, then you should dispute the item if you are not sure and return before the time expires. Once it has expired, you can message the vendor directly asking for help.

Do I have to pay for shipping on my return?

Back to top
The seller specifies this.

My item was listed as non-returnable, is there anything I can do?

Back to top
Special circumstances may arise such as receiving a broken or misrepresented. Use the "customer service" button on your order to ask a representative for help.
