Are there any fees for a buyer?

Back to top
The buyer is responsible for no transaction fees on Clearnet Market. The only fee a buyer will incur is wallet fees. They are universal with digital currencies and miners get there part.

Can you explain the wallet fee?

Back to top
Currently the fees are set dynamically. We calculate the average of fees sent in the last few hours and use that as our wallet fee. Everyone pays the same fees to withdraw as calculated by our site.

I have leftover coins but its smaller than the required txt fee, how do I get them off?

Back to top
Currently you cannot withdraw if your amount is less than the walletfee.
