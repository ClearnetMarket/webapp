What is Bitcoin?

Back to top
Bitcoin Cash is a currency, specifically a crypto-currency. It is peer-to-peer and decentralized, meaning it takes place without the need for banks, institutions, and goverment regulations. Its a means of people to make transactions in this world without the barrier of there local currency rates.
