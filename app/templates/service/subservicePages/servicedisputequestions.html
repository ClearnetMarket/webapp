Who keeps the fee during a dispute?

Back to top
The customer never pays a fee while on Clearnet Market(except wallet txt fees for withdrawls). If a dispute is issued with an unsuccessful trade, no fee is applied to the vendor. Fees are only applied upon successful trades and purchases.

Can I pursue action after a dispute has been settled?

Back to top
Once a dispute has been settled by a moderator, it is final. You can read our terms and conditions that you agreed upon when signing up to go over official actions and consequences.

How are disputes evaluated?

Back to top
Disputes are evaluated as is per dispute basis. We judge the overall conversation, proof of documents, receipts, feedback, etc to determine an outcome of a dispute.
