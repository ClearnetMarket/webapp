What are levels and EXP?


Each time your help, shop, buy, sell on Clearnet Market you get experience. Each amount of experience you have gained is reflected in your level.

Ways you can obtain experience and level up is by:

  • Buying something on Clearnet Market
  • Selling something on Clearnet Market
  • Giving feedback or finding bugs
  • Leaving vendor feedback
  • Receiving great feedback. The better the feedback, the higher the experience.
  • Trading items
  • Trading bitcoin
  • Donating to great causes we sponsor
  • more to come..

You can also lose feedback by:

  • Going through disputes
  • Receiving bad feedback
  • more to come..just you wait


What are trust levels?


Trust levels are obtained by vendors by holding an escrow with the site in-case of an incident. They help verify the vendor is serious and can help build trust at low levels. A vendor can give up the badge and receive there escrow back. It is optional for a vendor to obtain a trust level.
